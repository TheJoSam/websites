About Us

We're the newest Member of the TheJoSam Family the Racing Division.
Our Story started not too long ago with Mountainbike Racing Technology and continued with Radio Controlled Cars and Drones. And as of 2024 also parts for Motorcycles and their caretaking. The Goal in our Products is the maximum performance and rich features so everyone can enjoy Racing Technology without going backrupt.

Our Company was build up by TheJoSam for branding Prototypes designed for Racing. As of our first steps in Engineering the 3D-Printing technology was our best friend and starting point for some Mods. Today you can get Custom Parts for RC, Biking and Motorcycles and you can even request development of some special part.

Mountainbikes

Our starting point and first Division we had. It started with small but anoying things and expanded to whole Tools and part modification. Mountainbikes still hold a big impact here at TheJoSam Racing and will surely expand soon enough.

RC

The biggest part of our engineering and still is! The Journey started with small add-ons and evolved to the development of full RC Cars. RC Cars and Drones are sadly an dying Division here at TheJoSam Racing but we will stay open for Request.

Motorcycles

Now the newest and most interesting part of all. Motorcycles. The Journey of developing tools for mountainbikes and the videography behind TheJoSam was the cause to further evolve with the evolving status here at TheJoSam Racing.
